# MyVastu

Upload your floor plan and get an instant Vastu score with room-by-room feedback, powered by Claude AI.

## What it does

MyVastu analyzes apartment floor plans against Vastu Shastra principles. Upload an image of your floor plan, specify which direction is north, and get a score out of 100 with detailed feedback for each rule.

## Rules evaluated

- **Entrance direction** — main door placement relative to compass direction
- **Kitchen placement** — ideal location in the south-east
- **Master bedroom direction** — preferred in the south-west
- **Bathroom placement** — avoiding auspicious zones
- **Brahmasthan** — keeping the center of the home open and unobstructed
